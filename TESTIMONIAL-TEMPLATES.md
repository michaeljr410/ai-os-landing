# Testimonial Request Templates — AI OS Blueprint

> **When to send:** Day 3 and Day 7 after someone claims/buys the system
> **Where:** DM (Instagram, Telegram, or text — wherever you talk to them)
> **Goal:** Get a quick video testimonial (15-60 seconds) or text testimonial you can screenshot

---

## Template 1 — Day 3 Check-In (Soft Touch)

**Subject:** Quick check-in

> Yo [Name] — just checking in. How's the AI OS treating you so far?
>
> Have you had a chance to install it and run a few things? Curious what hit first for you.
>
> No rush — just want to make sure you're good.

---

## Template 2 — Day 7 Testimonial Ask (Direct)

**Subject:** Quick favor — 30 seconds

> [Name] — real talk. You've had the system for a week now. If it's been doing what I said it would, I need one thing from you.
>
> A quick testimonial. Doesn't have to be fancy — 15-30 seconds on camera, or even a text I can screenshot. Whatever's easiest.
>
> Here's what to cover (pick 1-2):
> - What were you doing before vs. now?
> - What's the first thing the AI did that surprised you?
> - Would you recommend it to someone in your circle?
>
> That's it. Your words help me get this in front of more people who need it.
>
> Drop it whenever you're ready. Appreciate you. 🤝

## Template 3 — For Cookout Members Specifically

**Subject:** The Cookout testimonial

> [Name] — you claimed the AI OS through The Cookout last week. How's it going?
>

## How to Use These

1. **Day 1:** They claim/buy → send nothing (let them explore)
2. **Day 3:** Send Template 1 (soft check-in)
3. **Day 7:** Send Template 2 or 3 (direct ask)
4. **Day 10:** If no response → send Template 4 (nudge)
5. **After testimonial received:** Send Template 5 (referral code)

## Where to Collect

- **Video testimonials:** Have them send via DM or text. Save to `~/Desktop/ai-os-landing/testimonials/` folder
- **Text testimonials:** Screenshot the DM. Save to same folder.
- **Usage:** Post on IG Stories, use in ads, add to landing page social proof section
